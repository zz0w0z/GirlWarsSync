# 💍 Marriage

{% hint style="success" icon="sparkles" %}
### Welcome to the Marriage Section.

**Marriage** is one of the type content the game as to offer. The game has **brides** in which you can unlock and then marry them, each **bride** gives stat boosts to specific heroes, specific hero types, and to all heroes. Here's a list of all the current/future **brides**, their stat boosts and how to unlock all the interactions they have to offer!

Other sources related to: **Bride**'s shard level chart.&#x20;

:exclamation:Notes: It is recommended to at least unlock them since the other venue (**Bond Festival**) to get them is p2w or it requires lots of months as a f2p to even unlock just one bride.
{% endhint %}
