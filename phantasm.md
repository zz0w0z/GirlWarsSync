<mark style="color:blue;">**\[Guardian Edge]**</mark><mark style="color:blue;">:</mark> Increases damage dealt by 25%, restores 50 Fury at the start of each ally's turn, lasts 2 turns, cannot stack. When dispelled, restores 100 Fury.

<summary><mark style="color:yellow;"><strong>Illusion Veil</strong></mark></summary>

When using a Normal Attack, Skill, ULT, Mega ULT, or Follow-up Attack, there is a 100.0% chance to inflict 1 stack of <mark style="color:$success;">**\[Reflected Moon]**</mark> on 2 random enemy heroes (prioritizing heroes without <mark style="color:$success;">**\[Reflected Moon]**</mark>).

When the wielder scores a Critical Hit on a single target with a Normal Attack, Skill, ULT, Mega ULT, or Follow-up Attack, deals additional damage equal to (This DMG \* 10 \* 5.85%) to 1 random enemy hero. If the target is affected by <mark style="color:$success;">**\[Reflected Moon]**</mark>, deals additional damage equal to (This Additional DMG \* 10 \* 5.85%) to another random enemy hero. This effect can trigger up to 2 times per single Critical Hit (the triggers from a single Critical Hit cannot target the same hero).

Each time this effect is triggered, the wielder gains 1 stack of <mark style="color:blue;">**\[Mirrored Bloom]**</mark>.

<mark style="color:$success;">**\[Reflected Moon]**</mark><mark style="color:$success;">:</mark> Reduces the target's Crit RES by 10% and Critical DMG RES by 15% for 2 rounds. Stacks up to 2 times.

<mark style="color:blue;">**\[Mirrored Bloom]**</mark><mark style="color:blue;">:</mark> Increases the wielder's Lifesteal by 10% and Crit Rate by 15% for 2 rounds. Stacks up to 2 times

<mark style="color:$success;">**\[Demonbreak]**</mark><mark style="color:$success;">:</mark> Attacks released by the effect holder have an 60.0%% chance to ignore 80% of the target's Defense, and apply 1 stack of <mark style="color:purple;">**\[Evil Dispel]**</mark> to the primary target. When attacked by an enemy hero with <mark style="color:purple;">**\[Evil Dispel]**</mark>, the effect holder recovers an additional 100 Rage. <mark style="color:blue;">**\[Blessing]**</mark> lasts until the end of the enemy's round, cannot stack, and cannot be dispelled.

<mark style="color:purple;">**\[Evil Dispel]**</mark><mark style="color:purple;">:</mark> Reduces the effect holder's DMG Up by 30%. Lasts for 2 rounds. Cannot stack.

<mark style="color:orange;">**\[Tainted Heart]**</mark><mark style="color:orange;">:</mark> Reduces the effect holder's DMG Reduction by 30%. Lasts for 2 rounds. Cannot stack.
