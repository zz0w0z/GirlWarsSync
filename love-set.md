The bonus % will reset at the start of the new turn.\
<mark style="color:blue;">\[Burst mode]:</mark> Enter burst mode on even turns, lasting for 1 turn.\

<mark style="color:purple;">\[Awakening Thunder]^:</mark> D\
<mark style="color:blue;">\[Burst]:</mark> +3\
